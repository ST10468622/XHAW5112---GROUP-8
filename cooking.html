Cooking Course

Course Overview

The six-week Cooking Course is designed to help learners gain confidence in the kitchen by mastering essential cooking techniques and understanding the basics of nutrition, hygiene, and meal planning. This course is practical, hands-on, and ideal for beginners or anyone looking to improve their culinary skills.



Duration:

6 weeks



Course Fee:

R750



Modules Include:

  • Kitchen safety and hygiene practices
  • Knife handling and food preparation techniques
  • Cooking methods (boiling, frying, grilling, baking, steaming)
  • Meal planning and portion control
  • Cooking nutritious and budget-friendly meals
