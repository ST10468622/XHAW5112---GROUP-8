Garden Maintenance



Course Overview

This six-week ensures trainees master the technical and seasonal skills required for professional, year-round garden health and upkeep. The curriculum is centered on understanding the fundamentals of horticulture, including proper soil conditioning, effective composting, and the controlled application of fertilizers for different plants. Key practical skills covered include precision pruning techniques, maintaining a pristine lawn and turf (mowing, edging, and weed control), and implementing basic, environmentally-friendly strategies for pest and disease management.



Duration:

6 weeks



Course Fee:

R750



Modules Include:

  • Water restrictions and the watering requirements of indigenous and exotic plants
  • Pruning and propagation of plants
  • Planting Techniques for different plant types
