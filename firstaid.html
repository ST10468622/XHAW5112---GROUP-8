First Aid Course

Course Overview

This six-month course focuses on providing certified, life-saving knowledge to manage accidents and emergencies in the home and garden with confidence. Trainees will gain practical skills in Basic Life Support (BLS), including performing CPR and managing choking for both adults and children. The training covers essential trauma care for common injuries like cuts, burns, and sprains, alongside recognizing and mitigating household hazards such as poisons and electrical risks. Successful completion equips staff to act as the primary responder until professional medical help arrives..



Duration:

6 months



Course Fee:

R1500



Modules Include:

  • Wounds and healing
  • Burns and fractures
  • Emergency scene management
  • Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation
  • Respitory Distress
