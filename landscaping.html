Landscaping Course

Course Overview

This six-month course is designed to elevate skills from simple maintenance to active property improvement, this module provides the aesthetic and practical knowledge for creating beautiful outdoor spaces. Trainees will be introduced to basic landscape design principles, focusing on plant selection, texture, color, and creating defined garden zones. Practical applications include learning correct planting and transplanting procedures for long-term plant health, as well as the essential upkeep of hardscape features like paving, decking, and basic irrigation systems.



Duration:

6 months



Course Fee:

R1500



Modules Include:

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees
  • Fixed Structures
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden
  • Aesthetics of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden Layout
